About Elegant Eventos

At Elegant Eventos, we craft experiences that are timeless, refined, and deeply personal. Established in 2020, our journey has been defined by a passion for transforming celebrations into unforgettable stories.

With a portfolio of 60+ meticulously curated events, we specialize in luxury wedding planning while also designing bespoke bachelorette soirées, birthday celebrations, baby showers, and corporate gatherings. Every event we create is a seamless blend of elegance, creativity, and precision — thoughtfully tailored to reflect the unique vision of our clients.

From conceptualisation to flawless execution, we offer end-to-end event planning services, ensuring every detail — from décor aesthetics to guest experience — is handled with sophistication and care. Our strength lies in our eye for detail, strong vendor collaborations, and the ability to turn even the smallest ideas into grand, immersive experiences.

At Elegant Eventos, we don’t just plan events — we design moments that linger long after the celebration ends.

About the Founder

I’m Kapil Navedia, founder of Elegant Eventos, based in the royal city of Udaipur, Rajasthan — a destination synonymous with grandeur and timeless celebrations.

What began in 2020 as a vision driven by passion has today evolved into a journey of creativity, growth, and meaningful connections. Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of working on diverse celebrations, gaining invaluable experience and the trust of wonderful clients.

My approach is simple — every event is personal. I believe in understanding each client’s story and translating it into an experience that feels effortless, elegant, and truly unforgettable. With a hands-on approach and a keen eye for detail, I ensure that every celebration carries a signature touch of sophistication.

For me, success is measured not just by beautifully executed events, but by the smiles, emotions, and memories we create along the way.
