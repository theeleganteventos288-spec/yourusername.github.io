About Elegant Eventos

At Elegant Eventos, we craft experiences that are timeless, sophisticated, and deeply personal. Founded in 2020, our journey has been fueled by a passion for turning dreams into celebrations that linger in memory long after the last toast.

With a portfolio of over 60 meticulously curated events, we specialize in luxury wedding planning, alongside bespoke bachelorette soirées, birthday celebrations, baby showers, and corporate gatherings. Every detail is thoughtfully designed to mirror the unique vision of our clients.

From concept to flawless execution, our end-to-end event services ensure every moment—from décor aesthetics to guest experiences—is handled with elegance, precision, and care. We pride ourselves on our keen eye for detail, trusted vendor collaborations, and the ability to transform even the smallest ideas into grand, immersive experiences.

At Elegant Eventos, we don’t just plan events—we create memorable moments that shine with timeless beauty and sophistication.

About the Founder

I’m Kapil Navedia, founder of Elegant Eventos, based in the royal city of Udaipur, Rajasthan — a destination synonymous with grandeur and timeless celebrations.

What began in 2020 as a vision driven by passion has grown into a journey of creativity, growth, and meaningful connections. Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of working on diverse celebrations, gaining invaluable experience and the trust of wonderful clients.

My approach is simple — every event is personal. By understanding each client’s story, I craft experiences that feel effortless, elegant, and truly unforgettable. With a hands-on approach and a keen eye for detail, every celebration carries a signature touch of sophistication.

For me, success is measured not just by beautifully executed events, but by the smiles, emotions, and memories we create along the way.
